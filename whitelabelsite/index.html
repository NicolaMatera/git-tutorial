Adaptive Viewing Experiences

Seamlessly switch between Focus View, Split View, and Chat-Only to match your workflow.

Focus view

Focus View is designed for deep concentration, offering a clean, distraction-free interface that keeps your content front and center. With integrated smart search, you can instantly locate information within a single file or across entire folders.

Split View, Chat-Only

Use Split View to chat and browse simultaneously, verifying AI insights side-by-side. Need speed? Switch to Chat-Only Mode for instant summaries across entire libraries. Both modes ensure pinpoint accuracy by focusing exclusively on your selected files.
